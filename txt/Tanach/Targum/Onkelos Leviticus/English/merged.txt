Onkelos Leviticus

Chapter 1

And he called to Moshe, and the Lord spoke with him from the Tent of Meeting saying:
Speak with the Children of Israel, and say to them "A man who offers from you (plural) the Offering before the Lord, from the cattle, from the oxen, and from the sheep, you (plural) will bring your offerings.
