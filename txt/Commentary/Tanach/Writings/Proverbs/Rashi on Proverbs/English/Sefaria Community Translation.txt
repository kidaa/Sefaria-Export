Verse 9

<b>From your wealth (<i>mehonkha</i>):</b> From all that He graced you with, even from a pleasant voice (do not read it as <i>mehonkha</i> [from your wealth] but [rather] as <i>megaronkha</i> [from your voice]). 
<b>And from the beginning:</b> These are the tithes to the priests and to the Levites.
