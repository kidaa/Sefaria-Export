Chapter 17









One may tear the covering that is on a [barrel] of wine or muryas [on Shabbos], as long as he does not intend to pour [from it]. One may not open sealed jugs [on Shabbos]; R' Shimon ben Gamliel permits [it].

Chapter 18
















One who bundles seeds to protect them from birds or melons to protect them from animals may bundle normally on shabbat as long as s/he does not clap, dance, or slap [thighs] as is done on a week day.
