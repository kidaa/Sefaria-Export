... He who wants to stand (or stick) to the literal translation of these texts, should be enlightened with this commentary. The people who came before me (i.e. other commentaries) did not understand anything. Moshe is responding to two things that Hashem said: 1. Go to Pharoah 2. Take the Jewish people out of Egypt based on the commandment of Pharoah. Moshe answers the first thing first, who am I to go to Pharoah even if i am just giving him a gift? Is it appropriate that I should go to the courtyard of the king as a foreigner like me? (Answering the second point) Even if i could go to visit Pharoah, I'm simpleminded and stupid towards other things, will Pharoah accept anything I say? Why would he listen to me?
