Tosefta Keilim Metsia

Chapter 1



3 Regarding a "klaustra" (i.e., door-hinge): Rabbi Tarfon declares it impure, but the Sages declare it pure. Beruriah says, "[One may] let it fall from this doorway and hang it on the neighboring doorway on Shabbat." When these words were spoken before Rabbi Joshua, he said, "Beruriah has spoken well (i.e., correctly)."
