Letter 1

א-<i>Aleph</i>, the first letter of the alphabet, interchanging with other gutturals, e. g. אבב, הבב, חבב, עבב; אליתא, עליתא &amp;c....
א- Often used to form second roots of verbs, ע"ע, e.g. טמם, אטם.
א- Frequent prosthetic, e. g. גודל, אגודל. See -אִ.
