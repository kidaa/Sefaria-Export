There was one man. This is the order: Moshe transmitted the torah to Joshua and from Joshua to the elders, and the elders to the judges, until Eli. And from there to Samuel like it says (Avot 1:1) "And the elders to the prophets."
From Ramathaim-zophim. There were two Ramots: zophot and raot. This is Yonatan in the Targum. Zophim, from the students of prophets?
Elkana. He was a Levite, in the line of Aviasaf son of Korach. As described in Chronicles I (5:8)....
An Ephraimite: Yonatan in Targum.
